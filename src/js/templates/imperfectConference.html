Imperfect Conference 2016

Men's Conference on February 20th from 8:30 AM — 12:15 PM featuring Ken Watson and Ben Watson. Ken is a native of Norfolk, VA, where he graduated form Lake Taylor High School. Ken is now the Senior Pastor at Rock Hill Bible Fellowship Church in Rock Hill. Ben is a tight end for the New Orleans Saints, a writer, and speaker. His first book, Under Our Skin, has just been released. This conference will be about what it means to be a man in today's society. This will be a great time of fellowship! Breakfast is included.
