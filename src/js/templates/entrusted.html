MAIN SESSION SPEAKERS

Nick and Kristle Davis

Kristle grew up to drug, alcohol and mentally affected parents. As the abuse in her family escalated, she sought help through the foster care system as a young teenager. Seven foster homes later, her forever family stepped in and dramatically changed her future.

Nick, having had an “all American” childhood, met Kristle during their college years and they married in 2004. Nick and Kristle suffered 2 miscarriages before they were blessed with 2 biological children.

After 10 years of engaging in a limited way, the Davises accepted their calling to foster care and adoption. They adopted a son in 2016 and use this story of coming full circle to establish church engagement in community outreach.
