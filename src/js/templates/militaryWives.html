Come join us twice a month for coffee, fun, and friendship with other women who understand the joys and challenges of being "mommy." This year we will be going through year 2 of Mom to Mom by Linda Anderson. No homework necessary - just a good time of meaningful conversation with new friends.

Crossroads Moms' Group meets from 9:30AM to 11:30AM on the 2nd and 4th Wednesday. Excellent childcare is provided in the church building. The new semester starts January 28th!

Please use our online form to register. For more information, please contact Christianne Page at ccpage@gmail.com.

 

Mission Statement:

Moms' Connection is a gathering of mothers of young children for the threefold purpose of:
   - Providing relationship building opportunities for moms and kids
   - Providing support to moms in the areas of child-raising, marriage, and homemaking
   - Providing encouragement for women to grow in their faith and relationship with Jesus Christ
