theCamp2016: June 27th - July 1st

Visit theCamp website to register: www.thecamp757.com

TheCamp is a high quality sports, arts, and bible camp for a week at the beginning of each summer that takes place in the inner city of Norfolk. It has become a major event in the life of CrossRoads Church - volunteers serving, loving and caring for campers. This is a primary mercy and justice mission for the church and acts as “VBS” for our CrossRoads kids. It has spawned summer-long activities that have proven to reduce the loss of learning that traditionally happens over the summer.

By ourselves, CrossRoads Church doesn't have the resources to serve this expanding ministry. We have partnered with Trinity Presbyterian, OV Church and several other local organizations to make theCamp a reality every summer.

All volunteers are required to attend a 2 hour training prior to the week of theCamp. Dates for this year are as follows:

  • June 19th 4 - 6PM at Trinity Presbyterian
  • June 21st 6 - 8PM at P.B. Young Elementary School
  • June 23rd 6 - 8PM at CrossRoads Church
