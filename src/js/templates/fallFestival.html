Fall Festival

Crossroads Church Annual Fall Festival! Sunday, October 30th from 5-7pm! Fun for all ages!! Chili Cook Off, Cake Walk, Bounce House, Prizes, Games, Yummy Treats, Crafts, Dunk Tank, AND MORE!

Costumes are welcome and encouraged! Please, nothing scary or inappropriate!
