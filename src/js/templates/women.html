HOPE Ministry

HOPE Ministry is a support group for wives of spouses who have- in the past or present- struggled with a sexual addiction. We provide a safe place for women to openly discuss their families' struggles through addiction and to find hope and healing. We will be using the book Addictions:a Banquet in the Grave as a springboard for discussion. Contact Caton Taylor at caton@crossroadsnorfolk.com.
