A vibrant church in Norfolk Virginia, CrossRoads is dedicated to furthering the Gospel of Jesus Christ through a commitment to the campus, community and world. We are comprised of people who want to see the city transformed by the love of God. We believe that every journey has CrossRoads and that God has a plan for every life. Our focus isn't building a large organization, but seeing people become fully devoted followers of Christ.
